# Move to a Folder
1. Click on *Home* ribbon and go to Quick Steps group.
2. Click dropdown, scroll down and select *New Quick Step* > *Move to Folder*
3. Enter a *Name*
4. *Move to folder*: Choose folder to move emails to.
5. Click *Finish*.
